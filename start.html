What do I need to start recording?

1) Computer/Laptop

As you start diving into the world of recording music at home, one of your first questions might be “what computer should I get?”

2) DAW Software

Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) is the software that is primarily used for recording music.

3) Audio Interface

In order to record audio efficiently, an Audio Interface is required.

4) Headphones

Headphones are essential if you want to hear what you're mixing! It's great for both at home and on the go.

5) Studio Monitors

Monitors are also essential for hearing your mixes. These tend to give you a better idea of volume levels than headphones do.

6) Microphone

Having a microphone is key to any studio setup!

7) Tools and Accessories

These are just minor things that can help spice up your studio!
